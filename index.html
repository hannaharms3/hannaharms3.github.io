Hannah Armstrong is currently a senior undergraduate student within the Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Science at the University of Miami, located in Coral Gables, Florida.

She will graduate with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Marine Affairs and Policy, as well as minors in Ecosystem Science and Policy and Visual Journalism, in May 2014.

Ms. Armstrong has participated in numerous oceanic research projects in and around the Caribbean, as well as South Florida, working with turtles, Lionfish, and most recently sharks.

Aside from her involvement in the marine sciences, she enjoys using graphic design and photography as a means of conveying issues that are problematic for and related to the world's oceans.
