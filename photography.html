Photography

I have enjoyed all aspects of photography and have been taking pictures for as long as I can remember. Throughout middle and high school I began delving into the field with black and white prints produced in a dark room. As my interest in the field continued to grow, I began experimenting with digital photography, grasping the basic concepts surrounding aperture and shutter speed. Most recently I have been completing assignments in the realm of "documentary photography", a form of photography aimed at telling stories, historical accounts and the like. My true passion lies in the field of underwater photography.

"Aftermath"

This picture was taken while doing a project titled "Boston Strong." This and the rest of the collection of pictures for this project capture areas in and around the city of Boston, Massachusetts following the 2014 marathon bombings, ultimately highlighting the resilience of the city while in recovery from such a tragic event.

"Surprised Eyes"

A Queen Conch keeping an eye on encroaching tropical fish and nearby divers.

Taken in the waters surrounding South Caicos in the Turks and Caicos Islands.

"Balancing Act"

Mariel Schofield, a former student athlete at the University of Miami, checks her blood sugar amidst one of her routine workouts.

This picture was taken for a depth of field project revolving around a student athlete living with Type 1 Diabetes. It aims to showcase both the struggles and triumphs she faces in her daily life.

"Flamingo Tongue"

A close-up of a Flamingo Tongue (Cyphoma gibbosum), a species of small yet vibrant sea snail, latched onto a Purple Sea Fan (Gorgonia ventalina).

Taken in the waters surrounding South Caicos in the Turks and Caicos Islands.

"Angles"

This picture was used in a project about downtown Miami, with much of the focus on Brickell, and specifically the metrorail and metro-mover.

"Gray Angel"

A Gray Angelfish (Pomacanthus arcuatus) swims alongside part of the Belize Barrier Reef.

Taken in the waters surrounding South Water Caye, a small island off the coast of Belize.

Smoke

This picture of a young woman blowing smoke was part of a project revolving around high key and low key light.
